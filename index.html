Something I like - cycling

I like cycling. It can be fast and fun. When I cycle at high speed, I feel like flying, seeing things flashing and moving fast toward my back side. It is very exciting.

I can cycle fast to places far away, allow me to see many more things than walking, do more physical exercises than driving. I can pass through narrow paths and woods that cars cannot.

It is convenient and simple to maintain a bike, only needing small space to store it, and little cost of keeping it operating. It costs little to own, maintain and repair a bike.

In all, I like cycling because it is:

  1. fast and exciting;
  2. convenient for travelling; and
  3. convenient for storage and maintenance.
